FK-32 Contiguity

[start]

[q]Define contiguity
[a]Contiguity - “The juxtaposition of two or more events when they occur simultaneously or very closely together (e.g. the succession of a response and a reinforcer in a superstition procedure or of a CS and a US in a respondent procedure)” (Catania, 1998, p. 383).
