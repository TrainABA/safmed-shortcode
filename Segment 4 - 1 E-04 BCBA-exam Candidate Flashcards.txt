E-04 Use Contingency Contracting
[start][q]Define contingency contracting[a]Contingency contract -  “…also called a behavioral contract, is a document that specifies a contingent relationship between the completion of a specified behavior and access to, or delivery of, a specified reward such as free time, a letter grade or access to a preferred activity” (Cooper, Heron & Heward, 2007, p. 551). [/qdeck]
