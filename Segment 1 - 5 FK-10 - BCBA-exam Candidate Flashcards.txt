SAFMED = Say All Fast, Minute Each Day
[start]

[q] Define behavior
[a] “The activity of living organisms; human behavior includes everything that people do” (Cooper, Heron, & Heward, 2007, p. 25).[q] Define response
[a] A single instance or occurrence of a specific class or type of behavior” (Cooper, Heron, & Heward, 2007, p. 703).  [q] Define response class
[a] “A group of responses of varying topography, all of which produce the same effect on the environment” (Cooper, Heron, & Heward, 2007, p. 703).
