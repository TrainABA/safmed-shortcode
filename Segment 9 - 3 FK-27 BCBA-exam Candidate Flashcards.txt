FK-27 Conditioned motivating operations
[start]

[q]Define surrogate CMO
[a]Surrogate CMOs replace and have the same effect as the motivating operation that it was previously paired with.

[q]Define reflexive CMO
[a]Reflexive CMOs create a circumstance in which its own removal serves as the reinforcement.

[q]Define transitive CMO
[a]Transitive CMOs make other stimuli more effective reinforcers.
