D-01 Use positive and negative reinforcement

[start]
[q]Define Positive Reinforcement
[a]Positive reinforcement has occurred when a stimulus is added to the environment, or is increased in intensity, immediately following a behavior, and this serves to increase the frequency of that behavior occurring in the future (Cooper, Heron & Heward, 2007).

[q]Define Negative Reinforcement
[a]Negative reinforcement has occurred when a stimulus is removed, or decreased in intensity, immediately following a behavior, and this increases the frequency of that behavior in the future (Cooper, Heron & Heward, 2007).
