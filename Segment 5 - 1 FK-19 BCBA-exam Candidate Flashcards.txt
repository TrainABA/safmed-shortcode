[q]Define unconditioned punisher
[a]Unconditioned punisher – “A stimulus change that decreases the frequency of any behavior that immediately precedes it irrespective of the organism’s learning history with the stimulus” (Cooper, Heron, & Heward, 2007, p. 707).
