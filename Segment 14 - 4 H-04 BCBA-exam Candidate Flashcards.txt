H-04 Evaluate changes in level, trend and variability

[start]

[q]Define level
[a]Level - “The value on the vertical axis scale around which a set of behavioral measures converge is called level (Cooper, Heron & Heward, 2007, pp. 150-151). 

[q]Define trend
[a]Trend - “The overall direction taken by a data path is its trend” (Cooper, Heron & Heward, 2007, p. 151).

[q]Define variability
[a]Variability - “How often and the extent to which multiple measures of behavior yield different outcomes is called variability” (Cooper, Heron & Heward, 2007, p. 150).
