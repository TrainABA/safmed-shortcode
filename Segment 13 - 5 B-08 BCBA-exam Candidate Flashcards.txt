[q]Define multiple probe designs
[a]Like multiple baseline designs (MBD), multiple probe designs (MPD) are “rigorous in their evaluation of threats to interval validity; and are practical for teachers and clinicians who want their research efforts to be wholly compatible with their instructional or therapy activities” (Gast, 2009, p. 277).
