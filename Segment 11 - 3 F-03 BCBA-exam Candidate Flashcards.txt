F-03 Use Direct Instruction

[start]

[q]Define direct instruction
[a]Direct instruction - a teaching method “…emphasizing the use of specified teacher directions, programmed instruction and presentation of materials, examples, and prompts, the use of reinforcement and mastery learning principles, regular and direct assessment, and teaching prerequisite skills.” (Callahan, Shukla-Mehta, & Wie, 2010, p. 78).
