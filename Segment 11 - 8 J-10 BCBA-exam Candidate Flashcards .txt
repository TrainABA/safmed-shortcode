J-10 When a behavior is to be decreased, select an acceptable alternative behavior to be established or increased. 

[start][q]Define DRA
[a]Differential reinforcement of alternative behavior (DRA) -  “a procedure for decreasing problem behavior in which reinforcement is delivered for a behavior that serves as desirable alternative to the behavior targeted for reduction and withheld following instances of the problem behavior” (Cooper, Heron & Heward, 2007, p. 693).
