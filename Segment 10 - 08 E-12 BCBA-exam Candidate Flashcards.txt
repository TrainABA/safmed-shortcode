E-12 Use Errorless Learning Procedures

[q]Define errorless learning
[a]Errorless learning - an “approach whereby the task is manipulated to eliminate/reduce errors. Tasks are executed in such a way that the subject is unlikely to make errors” (Fillingham, Hodgson, Sage, & Ralph, 2003, p. 339).
