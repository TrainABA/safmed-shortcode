FK-18 Conditioned reinforcement

[start][q]Define conditioned reinforcer
[a]Conditioned reinforcer - “A stimulus change that functions as a reinforcer because of prior pairing with one or more other reinforcers; sometimes called secondary or learned reinforcers” (Cooper et al., 2007, p. 692). 

[q]Define conditioned reinforcement
[a]Conditioned reinforcement – “the operation, or process, of a response producing a conditioned reinforcer that increases the likelihood that response occurs in the future” (Cooper et al., 2007, p. 40).
